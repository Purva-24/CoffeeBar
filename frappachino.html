Frappachino

Frappuccino is a line of blended iced coffee drink.

It may consist of coffee or crème base, blended with ice and ingredients such as flavored syrups and usually topped with whipped cream and/or spices.

It may also include blended Starbucks refreshers. Frappuccinos are also sold as bottled coffee beverages in grocery stores, convenience stores and from vending machines.

Ingredients:

    1. Espresso
    2. Ice
    3. Sweetner
    4. Flavored syrup
    5. Vanila extract
    6. Whipped Cream
    7. xanthan gum
