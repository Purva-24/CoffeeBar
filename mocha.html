Mocha

Mocha coffee is a chocolate-flavoured coffee beverage that combines espresso, steamed milk, and chocolate, often served hot or iced

It is a popular variant of a caffè latte, with the addition of chocolate syrup, cocoa powder, or melted chocolate, creating a rich, creamy, and indulgent drink

Ingredients:

    Espresso
    Milk
    Syrup
    Whipped Cream
    Chocolate Shaving
