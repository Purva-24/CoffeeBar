Mochafrappe

A mocha frappe is a chilled, blended coffee drink that combines chocolate and coffee flavors, often served with whipped cream and chocolate shavings.

It is a popular frozen beverage known for its smooth, frothy texture and rich taste, commonly enjoyed as a refreshing treat during warmer months.

Ingredients:

    1. NESCAFE Classic
    2. Hot Water
    3. Chocolate Syrup
    4. Vanila Extract
    5. Ice Cubes
    6. Cream
    7. Whipped Cream
    8. Chocolate Shaving
