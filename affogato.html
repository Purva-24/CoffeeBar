Affogato

Affogato is a classic Italian dessert made by pouring hot espresso over cold gelato or ice cream, creating a delightful contrast of temperatures and textures.

t is often enjoyed as a simple yet indulgent treat, especially after dinner.

Ingredients:

    1. Vanila gelote or ice cream
    2. Hot espresso
    3. Shaved Choclate
    4. Pinch of a sea salt
    5. Biscotti
